About School

Govt. Higher Secondary School, Alot Jageer is a premier educational institution in Ujjain district. Our mission is to provide quality education to rural and urban students, shaping their bright future.

School Overview:

  • Board: Madhya Pradesh Board of Secondary Education (MPBSE), Bhopal
  • Medium: Hindi
  • Classes: Class 9th to 12th
  • Streams Available: Science (Maths/Bio), Arts, and Agriculture
  • Timing: 10:30 AM to 4:30 PM

Our Facilities:

  • Modern Science and Computer Laboratories
  • Digital Smart Classrooms
  • Large Playground and Sports Equipment
  • Government Scholarship Facilities
  • Clean Drinking Water and Ventilated Classrooms
